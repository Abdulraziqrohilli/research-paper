About us

Welcome to our academic project website!

We are a group of undergraduate students from Information System, Kabul University, currently enrolled in the NLP(Natural Language Processing) course under the guidance of Dr. Wasi. This website has been developed as part of our university assignment, where we were tasked with reviewing, analyzing, and presenting multiple research papers related to Natural Language Processing].

The primary objective of this project was to deepen our understanding of how academic research is conducted, communicated, and applied. Each of us was responsible for reading and interpreting several research papers, identifying the key contributions, understanding the methodologies used, and evaluating the impact and limitations of the work. After a thorough review, we created visual slide presentations to summarize each paper and communicate our understanding in a clear, academic format.

Through this process, we've improved not only our ability to critically evaluate scientific literature but also our skills in summarizing complex information, collaborating as a team, and presenting technical content effectively. This project also introduced us to digital presentation tools and static website development, helping us bridge the gap between academic study and real-world communication platforms.

This website showcases the following:

  • A list of research papers we reviewed.
  • Summaries and insights drawn from each paper.
  • Our presentation slides.
  • Reflections and lessons learned during this project.
  • An introduction to our team and our individual roles.

We hope this site will serve as a helpful resource for fellow students, instructors, and anyone interested in understanding key ideas from the research papers we reviewed. It also stands as a record of our hard work and academic growth over the semester.
